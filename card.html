DALMATIAN

Dalmatian stains are like finger­prints, which usually appear three or four weeks after birth.

  • Scientific name: Canis lupus familiaris.
  • Average lifespan: 10-13 years old.
  • Origin: Croatien.
  • Habitat: varied.

Dalmatians are quite playful, in­telligent, outgoing, energetic, fri­endly, active and sensitive. Females can measure between 56 and 58 cm and males be­tween 58 and 61 cm. Fema­les weight between 16 and 24kg, while males 15 and 32kg. Dalmatians have a smooth short coat. Their coat has a white background, and can have black or liver sposts.
